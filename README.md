# Munshi Jee

A business and wholesale management system built for local traders and shopkeepers. Munshi Jee digitizes the traditional paper "khata" (ledger) into a simple, reliable tool for tracking accounts, inventory, and payments.

**Live site:** https://munshi-jee-production-0cb7.up.railway.app

---

## What it does

- **Digital khata / ledger** — track customer and supplier accounts in one place
- **Payment tracking** — record credits, debits, and outstanding balances
- **Inventory management** — keep stock and product records organized
- **Reminders** — stay on top of pending payments and dues
- **Built for local businesses** — designed around how wholesale traders actually work
